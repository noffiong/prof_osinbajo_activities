Where is Osinbajo?

Prof. Yemi Osinbajo has been working and ensuring the governance of Nigeria keeps running despite any limitations. Below are some of the activities of the Vice President for the month of March 2021

  • March 8: Attended Women in Africa Initiative webinar

  • March 10: Attended the National Security Council meeting presided over by President Buhari

  • March 13: Delivered a virtual tribute in honour of Prof. Galadima

  • March 16: Received a courtesy visit of Director General of World Trade Organization

  • March 17: Attended the National Security Council meeting presided over by President Buhari

  • March 18: Presided over National Economic Council meeting where he launched PEBEC Ease of doing business national baseline survey and he also declared open National Council for Privitization retreat

  • March 22: Met with Transport Association beneficiary of MSMEs Survival Fund, Met with private schools owners who benefited from MSMEs Survival Fund
